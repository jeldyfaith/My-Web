About Me

Annyeonghaseyo! I'm Jeldy Faith Ramirez, 22 years old from BSIT 601. I’m someone who finds joy in the simple and beautiful moments of life especially watching and capturing sunsets. There’s something peaceful and inspiring about the colors of the sky as the day ends, and I love preserving those moments through photos. I also value making others feel included and appreciated. I believe that kindness and connection can make a big difference, and I always try to create a positive and welcoming space for the people around me. At the same time, I’m learning to take care of myself and do the things that make me genuinely happy. When it comes to music, I enjoy listening to artists who inspire me and reflect my emotions. One of my favorite artists is Taylor Swift, whose music I connect with because of its meaning and vibe. Their songs often help me relax, think, and feel understood.
